How To Read It

Revenue

Total sales over the period. Bigger bars mean a larger business by sales.

Operating Income

Profit from the main business before interest and taxes. It shows core earnings power.

Operating Margin

A percentage that shows how much operating profit is kept from each dollar of revenue.
